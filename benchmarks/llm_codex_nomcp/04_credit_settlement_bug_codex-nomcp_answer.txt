Before the fix, `isNoCharge` was not actually enforced in settlement. The controller even populates `isNoCharge = 0` in the payload at [OrderController.php:848](/Users/naman/Documents/QBApps/qbapi/app/Http/Controllers/OrderController.php#L848), but `creditSettlement()` did not use that field. That meant `isNoCharge=1` orders could still enter settlement, and settlement would process them like normal credit-sale orders: reduce `PAYMENT_CREDIT`, insert new payment/accounting rows, and update balances. That is the double-counting path.
